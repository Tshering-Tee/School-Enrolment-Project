Sherabtse College

Sherubtse College, nestled in the serene surroundings of Kanglung, Trashigang District, is a venerable institution of higher learning in Bhutan. Established in 1966, it has evolved into a premier liberal arts college, offering a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Renowned for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education, Sherubtse College fosters intellectual growth, cultural enrichment, and social responsibility among its students. With a dedicated faculty and a vibrant campus community, the college provides a nurturing environment for students to excel academically, engage in extracurricular activities, and contribute meaningfully to society. Sherubtse College continues to uphold its legacy as a beacon of educational excellence, shaping the future leaders of Bhutan and beyond.

Royal Thimphu College

RTC, or Royal Thimphu College, situated in Thimphu, Bhutan, stands as a beacon of higher education excellence. Established in 2008 in collaboration with the Royal Government of Bhutan and the Royal University of Bhutan, RTC offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields such as social sciences, humanities, business, and environmental studies. With its emphasis on holistic education, RTC fosters critical thinking, cultural understanding, and leadership skills among its students, preparing them to become responsible global citizens.

College Of Natural Resources

CST, or College of Science and Technology, located in Rinchending, Phuentsholing, Bhutan, is a prominent institution dedicated to advancing scientific education and research. Established in 2001 under the Royal University of Bhutan, CST offers undergraduate programs in engineering disciplines such as civil, electrical, electronics and communication, and information technology. With modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, CST provides students with a comprehensive education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Committed to fostering innovation and technological advancement, CST plays a crucial role in shaping the future of Bhutan's engineering workforce and contributing to the nation's socio-economic development.

Taktse College of Language and Cultural Studies

Taktse College of Language and Cultural Studies, nestled in the serene valley of Trongsa, Bhutan, is a distinguished institution dedicated to the preservation and promotion of Bhutanese language, culture, and heritage. Established in 2008 under the Royal University of Bhutan, Taktse College offers undergraduate programs in Bhutanese language, literature, history, and cultural studies. With its unique focus on Bhutanese traditions and values, Taktse College provides students with a deep understanding of the country's rich cultural heritage and its significance in contemporary society. Through immersive learning experiences, research projects, and community engagement initiatives, Taktse College cultivates a new generation of scholars and cultural ambassadors, committed to preserving and celebrating Bhutan's unique identity on both national and global stages.
