UNIVERSITY OF BHUTAN

The University of Bhutan is the premier institution for higher education in Bhutan, offering a diverse range of academic programs and research opportunities. It comprises several colleges and institutes spread across the country, catering to various fields of study such as arts, sciences, engineering, and medicine. With a focus on holistic education and cultural preservation, the University of Bhutan plays a vital role in shaping the future leaders and professionals of the kingdom.

Sherubtse College

Sherubtse College, located in Kanglung, Bhutan, is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher education in the country.

Royal Thimphu College

RTC College in Bhutan is a leading institution for vocational education, providing hands-on training and industry partnerships for career-ready graduates.

Gyelpozhing College

Gyelpozhing College in eastern Bhutan offers undergraduate programs in arts, sciences, and commerce, fostering academic excellence and holistic development for students to contribute to Bhutan's progress.

College of Science and Technology (CST)

With a focus on practical skills and industry relevance, CST College equips students with the knowledge and expertise needed to thrive in today's dynamic digital landscape.
